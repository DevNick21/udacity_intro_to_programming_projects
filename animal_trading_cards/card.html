Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are renowned for their friendly and sociable nature, making them one of the most popular family pets in the United States. They are not only affectionate but also incredibly intelligent. What's particularly intriguing is their origin

  • Name: Golden Retriever
  • Average height: 21.5 to 24 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 12 years
  • Breed Origin: Scotland

Golden Retrievers are medium to large-sized dogs known for their friendly and outgoing nature. They stand 21.5 to 24 inches tall at the shoulder, with a weight range of 55 to 75 pounds. Their stunning double coat is a lustrous golden color, and their expressive, dark brown eyes radiate warmth and intelligence. With a well-proportioned head and a trademark friendly grin, they exude an approachable charm. These dogs are not only physically attractive but also possess a gentle and loyal temperament, making them popular family pets and excellent companions for people of all ages.
